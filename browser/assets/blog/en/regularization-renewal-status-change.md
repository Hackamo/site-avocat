## üìã Regularization Through Work

### Conditions to Fulfill

To obtain regularization through work, you must justify:

- **Length of stay in France** of at least 5 years (3 years for parents of schoolchildren)
- **Employment promise or work contract** in a so-called "shortage" occupation
- **Republican integration** (French language proficiency, respect for values of the Republic)
- **Absence of threat to public order**

### Occupations in Shortage

Certain sectors are actively recruiting and facilitate regularization:

- Building and civil works
- Hospitality and catering
- Services to individuals
- Agriculture
- Commerce and sales

### The File to Constitute

A regularization file must be complete and solidly argued:

- Residence permit application form
- Identity and address proofs
- Evidence of presence in France (receipts, invoices, attestations)
- Employment promise with training commitment if necessary
- Favorable opinion from DREETS (formerly DIRECCTE)
- Integration justifications (diplomas, training attestations, volunteering)
- Attestations from known and honorably known persons

## üîÑ Residence Permit Renewal

### Anticipate the Application

The renewal application must be filed **at least 2 months before expiration** of the current title. Delay can result in a period of irregular stay.

### Required Documents

- Current valid residence permit
- Updated proof of resources
- New employment contract or employer attestation
- Recent proof of address
- Compliant identity photos
- Tax stamps

### Special Cases

#### Change of Situation

If your situation has changed (marriage, birth, job loss), additional documents will be necessary.

#### Renewal Refused

In case of refusal, you have **2 months** to contest the decision. Martinet Law Firm assists you in this appeal procedure.

## üéØ Status Change

### What is a Status Change?

Status change consists of moving from one residence permit to another due to an evolution in your personal or professional situation.

### Main Status Changes

#### From Visitor to Employee

If you entered with a long-stay visitor visa and have found employment, you can request a status change. Conditions:

- Employment promise in an occupation in shortage
- Favorable opinion from DREETS
- Sufficient resources
- Proven integration

#### From Student to Employee

After your studies in France, you can obtain an employee title if:

- You obtained at minimum a Master's degree or equivalent
- Your job corresponds to your training
- The salary is at least equal to 2,080 times the minimum hourly wage
- The employer justifies not having found a candidate on the European job market

#### Status Change for Private and Family Life

If you live as a couple with a French or European national, or if you have family links in France, you can request a "private and family life" card.

### Procedures and Timeline

Status change is a lengthy procedure (6 to 12 months on average). It is imperative to prepare the file carefully from the start to avoid refusals.

### Refusal of Regularization or Renewal

If the prefecture refuses your application, you have several options:

1. **Gracious recourse**: ask the prefecture to re-examine your file
2. **Hierarchical recourse**: appeal to the Ministry of Interior
3. **Contentious recourse**: appeal to the administrative court

### Refusal of Status Change

The refusal must be motivated. If the reasons invoked are incorrect, an appeal before the administrative court can result in cancellation of the decision.
